Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a typical Asian dish that consists of white rice, your choice of meat and vegetables, eggs, and scallions that are all fried together with cooking oil. This dish is great for a light lunch or dinner with friends and family because you can cook a big batch. It is also great when you don't have the energy to cook food, and fried rice is a go to dish to whip up because it only takes about 10 minutes to cook. Although fried rice is a apprecited home meal, there are many restaurants around Asia serving a variety of fried rice such as shrimp fried rice, beef fried rice, seasme oil fried rice, and more. This version of fried rice is only one way of making it, and definitely explore and create your own fried rice!

Ingredients

  • Rice of choice (I will use white rice)
  • Eggs
  • Bacon
  • Scallion
  • Choice of oil (I will use bacon fat from bacon)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mince the scallions and set aside
  2. Add rice in a mixing bowl
  3. Add eggs and mix until the rice and eggs are well combined
  4. Set step 2 aside
  5. Next in a cold pan add in bacon (how many you want)
  6. Cook bacon until crispy at medium heat
  7. Once cooked grab a heat safe bowl and pour the bacon fat into the bowl
  8. Chop up the bacon and set it aside
  9. Now we will cook the egg and rice mixture. Turn the heat on at medium heat and pour half of the bacon fat onto the pan. Once the oil is hot, pour the rice mixture into the pan.
  10. Cook until egg is half cooked
  11. Turn off the heat
  12. Add in scallion and bacon you chopped earlier
  13. Mix until evenly combined
  14. Add salt and pepper to taste
  15. Transfer fried rice into a big plate to serve and eat
