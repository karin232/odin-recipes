Homemade Mochi

Decription

Mochi is a well known food that is made all over Asia. In Japan, they call it mochi and is made with sweet sticky rice. There are various kinds of mochi that are served during tea time or even special occasions. Mochi is simple but takes lots of love and labor to make one because they take time to combine. People now use mochi machines to cut the time, but mochi pounded by hand is much enjoyable. This recipe requries your mucsles to pound your mochi.

Ingredients and Equipments

  • Sweet sticky rice
  • Water
  • Rice Cooker
  • Rolling pin
  • Small bowl
  • Mixing bowl
  • Potato startch

Steps

  1. Measure three cups of sticky rice and pour it in the rice cooker
  2. Measure water until it hits the three cup line in the rice cooker
  3. Press cook and wait
  4. In the meantime, preapre a large mixing bowl and put a towl under, rolling pin, and small bowl of water
  5. When the rice is done cooking, immediately transfer it in the large mixing bowl
  6. Dip the rolling pin into the small bowl of water and start pounding the rice. If the rice starts to stick to the rolling pin, dip it in the water again.
  7. Repeat and pound until you don't see grains of rice
  8. Transfer mochi onto a tray with potato startch
  9. Separate mochi into 4 - 5 servings
  10. Eat!
